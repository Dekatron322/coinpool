About MetaFarm

Metafarm token is more than a metaverse based platform for advanced cryptofinal products, powered by the Metis ecosystem. Metafarm is the easiest way to explore the Metaverse and DEFI ecosystem with your assets on the Metis Ecosystem.

Here, you can farm new tokens with minimal trans- action fees and lower latency than any dapp on Ethereum, all while knowing that you’re getting a fair price. Additionally, you can also stake your NFTs on Metafarm. Metafarm does not hold your assets. You always retain full ownership of your account and all your synthetic assets.
